## Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. Go to [https://cinemai-pro-agents.vercel.app](https://cinemai-pro-agents.vercel.app)
2. Click **"Start Free"**
3. Enter your email and create a password, OR sign in with a social account
4. Accept the **Likeness & Consent Policy** (required to use avatar features)

### Connect Social Accounts

To publish campaigns automatically, connect your social accounts:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Social Connections**
2. Click **"Connect Twitter/X"** and authorize the app
3. Click **"Connect Farcaster"** and authorize the app

## Creating Scripts

The **Script Agent** generates professional video scripts tailored to your product and audience.

### Step 1: Start a New Script

1. Navigate to **Scripts** → **New Script**
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Product Description**: Describe your product or service
   - **Target Audience**: Who is your ideal customer?
   - **Tone**: Professional, casual, urgent, friendly, etc.
   - **Duration**: 30s, 45s, 60s
   - **Template** (optional): Product Launch, UGC Style, Testimonial, Tutorial

### Step 2: Generate Script

Click **"Generate Script"**. The AI will create a structured script with:
- **Hook**: Opening line to capture attention
- **Body**: Main content organized in sections
- **CTA**: Call-to-action at the end

### Step 3: Edit & Save

- Review and edit the generated script
- Click **"Save Script"** to store it in your library

## Generating Videos

The **Video Agent** turns your scripts into videos with virtual presenters.

### Step 1: Select a Script

1. Navigate to **Videos** → **New Video**
2. Select a script from your library

### Step 2: Choose Avatar & Settings

- **Avatar Style**: Select a virtual presenter style (e.g., professional, casual, animated)
  - **Important**: Only use fictional or stylized avatars. Do NOT request likenesses of real people.
- **Language**: Choose the language for voiceover
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (landscape), 9:16 (vertical), or 1:1 (square)

### Step 3: Generate Video

Click **"Generate Video"**. The video will be queued for processing.

- **Status**: "Queued" → "Processing" → "Ready"
- You'll receive an email when the video is ready
- Processing typically takes 5-15 minutes depending on length

### Step 4: Download or Create Campaign

- **Download**: Click the download button to save the video
- **Create Campaign**: Click **"Create Campaign"** to schedule social posts

## Launching Campaigns

The **Campaign Agent** schedules and publishes your videos across social platforms.

### Step 1: Start a Campaign

1. From a ready video, click **"Create Campaign"**
2. OR navigate to **Campaigns** → **New Campaign**

### Step 2: Configure Campaign

- **Select Video**: Choose the video to publish
- **Platforms**: Select Twitter/X and/or Farcaster
- **Caption**: The AI generates platform-specific captions, or write your own
- **Schedule**: Publish now or schedule for later

### Step 3: Review & Launch

- Review all settings
- Click **"Launch Campaign"**

### Step 4: Monitor Results

- Go to **Campaigns** to see status and logs
- View analytics in **Dashboard** → **Analytics**

## Managing Social Accounts

### Social Timeline

See all your recent posts in one place:

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Social Timeline**
2. View posts from all connected accounts
3. Filter by platform or source (manual vs. agent-created)

### Disconnect Account

1. Go to **Settings** → **Social Connections**
2. Click **"Disconnect"** next to the account you want to remove

## Billing & Plans

### Plans

- **Free**: Limited scripts, no video generation, no auto-posting
- **Pro** ($49/month): Unlimited scripts, 10 videos/month, auto-posting, analytics
- **Scale** ($199/month): 50 videos/month, priority processing, white-label options

### Upgrade

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **"Upgrade to Pro"** or **"Upgrade to Scale"**
3. Enter payment details via Stripe
4. Your new limits take effect immediately

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"** → Opens Stripe Customer Portal
3. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
4. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period

### View Usage

Track your usage in **Dashboard**:
- Scripts created this month
- Videos generated this month
- Campaigns launched this month

## FAQ

### Can I use real people in my videos?

**No**, unless you have explicit, documented consent from the person. Our platform enforces strict likeness and consent policies. You must:
- Use fictional or stylized avatars
- Avoid impersonating public figures
- Include clear disclosure that content is AI-generated

Violations may result in account suspension.

### How are videos generated?

We use AI video generation providers to create videos with virtual presenters. The process involves:
1. Analyzing your script
2. Selecting an avatar and voice
3. Synthesizing video and audio
4. Rendering the final output

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Manage Subscription** and click "Cancel Subscription" in the Stripe portal.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

- Your scripts and videos remain accessible for 30 days
- After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted
- You can export your content before canceling

### Can I request a refund?

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact support at support@cinemai.ai with your request.

### How do I export my videos?

1. Navigate to **Videos**
2. Click the download icon next to each video
3. Videos are downloaded as MP4 files

### Do you offer white-label options?

Yes, the **Scale** plan includes white-label options. Contact sales@cinemai.ai for custom branding.

### How do I report a bug or request a feature?

- **Bugs**: Email support@cinemai.ai
- **Feature Requests**: Submit via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/SMSDAO/Cinemai/issues)
